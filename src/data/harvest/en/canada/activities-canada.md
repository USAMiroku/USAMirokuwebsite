Summary of typical activities and services offered by Miroku Association
Canada (Johrei Canada), based on public site information:

- Johrei practice: local group sessions where Johrei is ministered and received.
- Faith experiences and testimonies shared on the site.
- Online prayer/request form to submit prayer requests.
- Educational resources and e-books (links to Amazon/Apple Books for Meishu-Sama teachings).
- Local gatherings, events and meetings organized by city groups and centres.
- Donation options for supporting local activities and the association.

For current event dates and schedules, check the site or contact canada@johrei.ca.
